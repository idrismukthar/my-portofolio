About Me

My name is Idris Mukthar. I am a passionate and multi-skilled creative based in Lagos, Nigeria. I specialize in software development, with a focus on creating user-friendly and visually appealing web applications. I have a strong foundation in front-end web development, mastering technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, and more. Beyond coding, I am also highly experienced in UI/UX design using Figma, creating seamless and visually appealing digital experiences.

My skills extend to professional video editing, with proficiency in tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, allowing me to produce engaging multimedia content. I am currently studying Microbiology at Lagos State University (LASU), combining my academic pursuits with my passion for technology and design. I believe in continuous learning, creativity, and building impactful solutions that connect with people.

My mission is to blend science, art, and technology to create innovative digital products. I am always open to new opportunities to grow, collaborate, and leave a mark in the digital space.
